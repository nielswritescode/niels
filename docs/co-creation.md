If you're reading this, I guess we might be cocreating something together. Yay!

I have been having various offers (both giving & receiving) lately and for the sake of efficiency, clarity and transparancy I'd like to be upfront about who I am as an organizer! Happy to hear about you upfront as well :)

These are not 100% strict boundaries - but they do give a good impression of who I am and my potential / limitations. 

- I am a **low energy** person. Fell on my head once, took a year in bed to recover - still having many issues. I live happily, but have clear energetic limits. I am mostly drawn to the **2-6 hour event** range (depending on intensity), and also like 30-60 min events.  
- Generally, sexuality is too big a trauma for me to organize anything around. Cuddling and physical touch are getting easier but still not my forte.
- I am mostly interested in hosting unconditional events for self growth. I don't have a perfect definition for unconditional, but important facets include the event being non-transactional, non-political and non-ideological. Self-growth definition is also stretchable but I tend to dislike stuff that is 'just for fun'. 
- I could also see myself being interested in other (not unconditional) types of events in any role. I would just have different boundaries and expectations. 

Examples of what I've hosted the past 5 years[~100 events total]:

- Folk dance festival
- Hide & Seek in the forest
- Authentic Relating workshops
- Mindful Walk
- Meditations
- Experiential Dance
- Mindful Improv Theater
- Somatic Nature Experiencing
- Public listening sessions
- Sharing circles
- Rejection therapy

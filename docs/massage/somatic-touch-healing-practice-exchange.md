## 🫙 Somatic Touch Healing

✨ Actually zero expectations  
✨ Silent  
✨ Set amount of time [default=30mins]  
✨ Both giver and receiver are invited into presence & open to not knowing.  
✨ Touch can be any kind of touch / massage within the boundaries.  
✨ Receiver can state what they would love to receive at the start - but the giver is not obliged to adhere to it. 

### Possible modifications _sky's the limit_

⚙️ Include verbal sharing from either the giver, receiver or both. 

## Why?

- There is an enormous touch-scarcity in the world. This is a no-experience-needed practice for touch.
- The touch that I most like to receive is touch where the giver is deeply present with both themselves and with me. In particular I like the giver to feel into the physically stored partition of my traumas and explore if it is possible to relieve them through external care. This is only possible through deep presence & attunement. 
- Zero expectations means we create a practice space for touch. I believe we need more practice spaces than performance spaces. There are social expectations around touch / massage - and this framework provides the safety to drop those.  
- The touch can be for the giver, for the receiver. But in this practice, the touch is likely to serve both. 

## 🌸 Sexuality
Unless otherwise discussed, I assume the massage is completely platonic. 
This can be an intimate practice - sexual activation is normal. I breathe, pause or stop depending on the level of activation. I expect you to do the same. 

## ❗Boundaries
✅ Naked massage.  
✅ Light sensual touch if it comes from a place of care.  
✅ Sitting on me.  
⛔ Genital touch.  
⛔ Sensual touch if it comes from a place of sexual activation.  
⛔ Touch with excessive force.

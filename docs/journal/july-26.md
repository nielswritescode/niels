The day started with an almost explosive energy of wanting to get lost. I truly was with the desire and consciously chose to dip it into coziness. There's so much that emerged yesterday, besides coziness...  
I decided to spend 3 hours under the bridge. Hard concrete. I feel safe. Or at least safer. It took me an hour into the thought space to arrive. Get bored, the meta-patterns telling me to go home. Just actually move past them. And I'm there - jumping on rocks, kicking walls. Listening to a song as big boats float by. Not giving in to the impulse to check the time multiple times. It felt powerful. What if I don't give in to impulses at all...? And what is behind the impulses. They are the subconscious patterns I'm unware of and actually have control over my life. It might not be me living, but just. those. patterns.
Time for the meeting. I took a right at a crossroads. Stopped. And went back to the crossroads. Went left. A spark emerged. One of great adventure. Also a lot of caution. In synchronicity they ride. I would have loved the meeting to.... be love. Yeah, mostly that. It instead didn't move very significant things, although... It seems to always move something. More hours, a sunset, a beach. A single 50second voice message to comfort me. A guy who was too shy to talk sitting on the rocks. Hiding my phone under the rocks in a sock. Climbing on all fours to the edge of the shoreline. Taking a swim and running in circles through the sand to get warm. Am I impressing people with my nakedness? A cat walks through the night. We're on the same wavelength now. There was no clear intent today. What happens without? I want to feel deeply into nature. I felt some deep feelings, got shaken. Made peace with the silence and the discomfort. But I didn't find the thriving I was looking for. I might tell myself I'm okay and that I don't strive to thrive. But I kinda do... and I need to thrive in order to stay motivated for practice. Cause practice is fucking hard. It really is. There's cold, there's sand, theres pain. There's surrender to the universe and not knowing how to do it. I managed a couple of times, which of itself is probably already quite absurd. I could probably be extremely proud of that. But I want it to be continuous. I mean, I want to be different anyhow. Alive. I'm so, not, alive right now. Relatively, yes. I love. But absolutely.... I'm okay with being exactly who I am. For now.

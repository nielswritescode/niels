I went to Diemerbos for a mindful walk. I sat down prior to starting. Let me really intentionally start the practice, instead of fleeing by 'hiding' in the practice. _I'll just do 30 minutes of mindfulness and then go back to 'safety' again._

30 minutes. A timer felt appropriate today. Being able to practice without a timer seems to have enhanced the practice with a timer. A thought passed by: Let me not pretend today. I did not. 

The practice ended. I found myself on a hot bridge. A medium burn on my feet. It's also just a sensation? After a minute I lifted my feet up and back down again. The heat seemed to be released from the pocket it was in. 

I went to the circle. Sat down, thought. Away from reality. Back again. I'm here, in this forest. I just came from a practice?

What if I did another one? I feel resistance. But actually I'd like to work with this resistance today. When I started, I felt so grateful. Working with resitance out of self-love instead of out of discipline. This seems to be the path I'm chasing. 

Second round was kind of awkward. Less feelings. There were some at some point. I saw a woman in the distance. Humans still have such impact on my NS... Look at her? Ignore her? Vow of silence? People-pleasing? Fake smiles? Weird guy in the forest? Collective suffering? A simple smile from a stranger? My actual discomfort? 

I rested with my actual discomfort and sadness. Let me experience that as she walks past. I can look at her without entering the social pattern, but without stopping it either. Let me truly invite her into my being, given the pracitce my being is currently in. Then it's up to her choice. I still haven't truly figured this out. But I seem to be getting closer.

The 30 minutes passed. I ran, barefoot. Laid down on a piece of wood. Just let the splinters enter my skin if they do. I don't care. Will get them out I guess. Am I going home? Yes. The practice has ended. It also never ends. A learning process. Sadness.

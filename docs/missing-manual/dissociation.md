# Trauma curve

I am always feeling a large part of my consciousness drawn to something unpleasant. 

I have described it as 'the trauma curve', 'a tension mostly from my upper back to my eye muscles'. 

It feels like there is an unnatural arrangement in my body. I imagine it to be a combination of hypertense muscles and the result of not activating/ having activated nerves in certain areas (like my chest fore example) does to the body. 

A fysio once told me I wasn't breathing enough in the chest.

Possible causes that I imagine:
